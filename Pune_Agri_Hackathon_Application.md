## PROJECT TITLE
**KisanVaani — Voice-First AI Farm Advisor for Smallholder Farmers**

## ONE-LINE DESCRIPTION
AI-powered voice assistant that lets farmers dial a phone number and get expert agricultural advice in their own language — no smartphone, no internet, no app needed.

## PROBLEM STATEMENT (200 words)
India has 150 million smallholder farmers, yet 86% have low digital literacy and most lack smartphones. Existing agri-tech solutions like apps and chatbots are inaccessible to the farmers who need them most.

The critical gap: farmers cannot get timely advice on pest identification, crop management, market prices, or government schemes because every existing tool requires a smartphone with internet access.

Result: Preventable crop losses worth Rs 90,000+ crore annually, delayed pest response, missed government benefits, and poor market timing that costs farmers 20-40% of potential income.

Even Bharat-VISTAAR (government's AI platform) launched only in Phase-1 with limited language support. No existing solution offers personalized memory, photo diagnosis, proactive alerts, multi-country support, and expense tracking in a single voice-first platform.

## SOLUTION (300 words)
KisanVaani is the world's first AI farm advisor that combines voice-first interaction, farmer memory, photo diagnosis, and proactive alerts in one platform.

**How it works:**
1. Farmer dials a phone number from ANY phone (even Rs 500 feature phone)
2. Speaks their question in Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, Swahili, or English
3. AI understands the question, analyzes it with farming knowledge, and responds with specific actionable advice — spoken back in the same language

**What makes KisanVaani unique (features NO competitor has):**

- **Farmer Memory**: Remembers each farmer's name, crops, soil type, land size, and past problems. "Welcome back Ramesh ji! How is your tomato crop since the whitefly problem?"
- **Photo Diagnosis**: Upload a crop photo via WhatsApp — AI analyzes leaf color, spots, and damage patterns alongside voice description
- **Proactive Alerts**: System warns farmers BEFORE problems hit — pest outbreaks, weather events, price spikes, scheme deadlines
- **Voice Expense Tracker**: "Maine 3500 rupye beej pe kharch kiye" → auto-logs expenses with category and crop
- **Community Intelligence**: Aggregates questions from nearby farmers to detect pest outbreak patterns — "47 farmers in your district reported the same pest this week"
- **Multi-Country**: Works across India, Kenya, Nigeria, and Ethiopia with country-specific schemes, prices, and extension services

**Technology:**
- Claude AI (Anthropic) for intelligent farming advice
- Browser Speech API for free speech-to-text
- Twilio for real phone calls
- ClinicalTrials.gov-style architecture: real-time data, not static databases

**Tested with real Claude AI**: Hindi pest diagnosis, Swahili drought advice, Telugu government scheme queries, English market analysis — all producing specific, actionable, farmer-friendly responses.

## IMPACT & SCALABILITY
- **Immediate**: 150M Indian smallholder farmers accessible via phone call
- **Year 1 target**: 10,000 farmers across 5 states
- **Scalable**: Same platform serves India, Kenya, Nigeria, Ethiopia — 500M+ total farmers
- **Health equity**: Rural farmers far from KVKs get instant expert advice
- **Economic impact**: 20-40% reduction in crop loss through timely pest/disease diagnosis

## TECH STACK
- Backend: Python FastAPI + SQLite
- AI: Claude (Anthropic) — multilingual farming advisor with Vision for photos
- Voice: Twilio (phone), Browser Speech API (web demo)
- Frontend: React + TypeScript + Tailwind CSS
- Data: Real-time crop calendars, market prices, government schemes for 4 countries
