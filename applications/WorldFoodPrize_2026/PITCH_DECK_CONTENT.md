## Slide 2: The Problem

**1.3 Billion Farmers Left Behind**

- 1.3B+ smallholder farmers produce 40% of world's food
- **Zero** have access to AI tools
- Every existing solution requires: smartphone + internet + English
- Result: **40% post-harvest losses** = $940B/year wasted

**The Irony:**
> AI can diagnose crop diseases in milliseconds, predict market prices, and monitor fields from space — but it can't reach the farmers who need it most, because they don't have smartphones.

## Slide 4: Our Solution

**KisanVaani: AI that speaks the farmer's language**

```
Farmer calls → Speaks in Hindi/Telugu/Tamil/Swahili/English
                    ↓
        Claude AI understands context
    (crops, soil, location, season, history)
                    ↓
        Specific, actionable advice
    "Spray neem oil 5ml/liter on leaf undersides every 3 days"
                    ↓
        Farmer hears answer in their language
```

**3 channels, 1 platform:**

## Slide 10: Business Model

| Revenue Stream | Model | Example |
|---|---|---|
| **Government contracts** | Per farmer/year | State ag dept pays $0.50-$1 per farmer |
| **Input companies** | Sponsored recommendations | Seed brand pays for product visibility |
| **Insurance facilitation** | Commission per claim | 2-5% of claim amount |
| **Marketplace fees** | Transaction fee | 2-5% of produce sold |
| **FPO subscriptions** | Premium features | Satellite + price prediction for cooperatives |

**Unit Economics:**
- Cost per interaction: **$0.02**
- Revenue per farmer/year: **$1-3**
- Gross margin: **70%+**
- Break-even: **50,000 active farmers**
