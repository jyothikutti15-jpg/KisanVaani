# KisanVaani — One Page Summary

## Voice-First AI Farm Advisor for Smallholder Farmers

---

### The Problem
1.3 billion smallholder farmers — producing 40% of the world's food — have zero access to AI. Every existing solution requires a smartphone, internet, and English. Result: 40% post-harvest losses ($940B/year).

### The Solution
KisanVaani lets farmers get expert agricultural advice through a simple **phone call, WhatsApp message, or SMS** — in Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, Swahili, or English. No app. No internet. No smartphone needed.

### How It Works
Farmer calls → Speaks question in local language → Claude AI understands crops, soil, season, history → Returns specific advice with exact dosages → Farmer hears answer read aloud.

### 22 Features in One Platform
**AI Advisory:** Voice chat, photo crop diagnosis, multi-turn memory, 5 languages
**Smart Farming:** Weather forecasts, crop diary, auto-reminders, yield prediction, satellite NDVI monitoring
**Financial Tools:** Expense tracking, AI price prediction (HOLD/SELL), loan eligibility, insurance auto-claims
**Access Channels:** Phone call, WhatsApp, SMS, web demo, expert human callback, farmer marketplace

### 3 Features No Competitor Has
1. **Soil Test Report OCR** — Photo of soil card → AI reads values → fertilizer plan
2. **Insurance Auto-Claims** — Generates PMFBY claims from diary + satellite + photos
3. **SMS Fallback** — Full AI farming advice via basic SMS, zero internet
