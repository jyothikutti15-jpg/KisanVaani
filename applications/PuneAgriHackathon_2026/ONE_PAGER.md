# KisanVaani v2.1 — Pune Agri Hackathon 2026

## Voice-First AI Farm Advisor | 22 Features | 5 Languages | No Smartphone Needed

---

### The Problem
150 million Indian smallholder farmers lose **Rs 90,000+ crore annually** to preventable crop losses. 86% lack smartphones. Every agri-tech app requires one. Extension services have 1 officer per 1,000+ farmers. Result: zero AI access for the farmers who need it most.

### The Solution
Farmer **dials a phone number** or sends **WhatsApp/SMS** → speaks in Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, Swahili, or English → **Claude AI** responds with specific, personalized advice → farmer hears answer in their language. Works on any Rs 500 phone.

### 3 Features That Exist Nowhere Else
1. **Soil Test Report OCR** — Photo of soil card → AI reads pH/N/P/K → fertilizer plan per acre
2. **PMFBY Insurance Auto-Claims** — Generates complete claim from diary + satellite + expenses
3. **SMS AI Fallback** — Full farming advice via SMS for zero-internet areas
