# KisanVaani — One Page Summary for UNDP timbuktoo AgriTech Programme

## Voice-First AI Farm Advisor for African Smallholder Farmers

---

### The Problem
65 million African smallholder farmers produce 80% of the continent's food but have zero access to AI tools. Every existing solution requires a smartphone and internet — excluding the 60-85% who use basic phones. Result: 40% post-harvest losses, $14B/year wasted across Sub-Saharan Africa.

### The Solution
KisanVaani delivers expert farming advice via **phone call, WhatsApp, and SMS** — powered by Claude AI (Anthropic's frontier model). No app. No internet. No smartphone needed. Available in **Swahili and English**, serving **Kenya, Nigeria, and Ethiopia**.

### How It Works
Farmer calls or texts → AI understands crop, season, location, and farmer history → Returns specific advice ("Mix neem oil 5ml + soap 2ml in 1L water, pour into the whorl every 3 days") → Farmer hears answer in their language.

### 22 Features, One Platform
**AI Advisory:** Voice chat, photo crop diagnosis (Claude Vision), multi-turn memory, 5 languages
**Smart Farming:** Weather forecasts, crop diary, satellite NDVI monitoring, yield prediction
**Financial Tools:** Expense tracking, AI price prediction, insurance auto-claims, loan eligibility
**Access:** Phone call + WhatsApp + SMS + expert human callback + farmer marketplace

### 3 Features No Competitor Has
1. **Soil Test OCR** — Photograph soil report card → AI reads and interprets values → fertilizer plan
2. **Insurance Auto-Claims** — Auto-generates claim packages from diary + satellite + photos (ACRE Africa, NAIC)
3. **SMS Fallback** — Full AI farming advice via basic SMS for zero-internet areas

### African Market
- Kenya: 4.5M basic-phone farmers (addressable)
- Nigeria: 24.7M basic-phone farmers
- Ethiopia: 12.75M basic-phone farmers
- Africa AgriTech market: $5.6B → $12.7B by 2030
