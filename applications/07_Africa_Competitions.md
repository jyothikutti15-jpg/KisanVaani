# Africa Competition Applications — KisanVaani

## A. Tony Elumelu Foundation ($5,000 seed capital)
**Website**: https://www.tonyelumelufoundation.org/
**Prize**: $5,000 non-refundable + training + mentorship

### Business Idea
KisanVaani provides AI-powered agricultural advisory to smallholder farmers via phone call in local languages. Targeting Kenya and Nigeria first.

### African Market Focus
- Kenya: Maize, beans, tea farmers in Western Kenya, Rift Valley
- Nigeria: Maize, rice, cowpea farmers in Kano, Kaduna, Ogun states
- Languages: Swahili, English (adding Hausa, Yoruba)
- Tested with real Kenyan/Nigerian farming data, prices in Ksh/Naira, local schemes (NARIGP, Anchor Borrowers)

### Revenue in Africa
- Telco partnerships (Safaricom Kenya, MTN Nigeria)
- County/state agriculture department contracts
- NGO funding (USAID, DFID, Gates Foundation)
- Agri-input company advertising
