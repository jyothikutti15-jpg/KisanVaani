## Application Templates

### One-Line Description
> KisanVaani: Voice-first AI farm advisor that lets smallholder farmers get expert agricultural advice through a simple phone call in their own language — no app, no internet, no smartphone needed.

### 50-Word Summary
> KisanVaani is a voice-first AI farming advisor serving smallholder farmers across India, Kenya, Nigeria, and Ethiopia in 5 local languages. Farmers call, WhatsApp, or SMS their questions and receive expert crop advice, disease diagnosis, price predictions, and insurance guidance — powered by Claude AI, satellite monitoring, and community intelligence.

### 200-Word Problem Statement
> Over 1.3 billion smallholder farmers — producing 40% of the world's food — have zero access to AI tools. Every existing agritech solution requires a smartphone, internet connection, and English fluency. Meanwhile, these farmers lose 40% of their harvest to preventable causes: wrong pesticide dosages, missed planting windows, undiagnosed diseases, and information asymmetry with middlemen.
>
> The consequences are devastating: $940 billion in annual post-harvest losses, deepening rural poverty, and food insecurity affecting 735 million people globally. Government extension services are understaffed (1 officer per 1,000+ farmers in India), and existing digital advisory tools only reach farmers who already have smartphones and data plans — the ones who need help least.
>
> KisanVaani solves this with a voice-first approach: farmers simply call a phone number or send an SMS. No app download. No internet. No English. Our AI advisor speaks Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, Swahili, and English, remembers each farmer's crops and history, diagnoses diseases from photos, predicts market prices, monitors crop health via satellite, and even auto-generates insurance claims. We've built the most comprehensive AI farming platform — accessible to every farmer with a basic phone.
